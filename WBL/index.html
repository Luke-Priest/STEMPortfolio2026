Internship

At the Dayton Regional STEM School, 11th-grade students are required to complete an 80-hour internship in their intended career field. This is an excellent hands-on experience working with professionals and seeing the practical aspects of a chosen path. My internship was here at DRSS, directly working with the school's IT department during the 10 days of STEMmersion for 8 hours each day. I gained exposure to the various facets of IT support, such as resolving hardware and software issues, and learning about the systems driving our learning environment.
