Greatest Growth : Communication

Through out my Highschool life, communication has definitly been my greatest growth.

Artifacts

9th Grade Project

As part of a collaborative class project, I participated in a mock trial where I served as defense counsel. Working with a co-counsel, I prepared and delivered an opening statement for our case, which required analyzing evidence, building persuasive arguments, and anticipating the opponents claims.


  • Public Speaking: Delivering arguments clearly and persuasively to an audience of my peers.
  • Persuasive Writing: Crafting an effective opening statement with logic and emotional appeal.
  • Confidence in Professional Communication: Learning how to present myself in a formal setting under pressure.

10th Grade Project

In Chinese 1, I collaborated with three classmates to create a rap video in Mandarin Chinese, showcasing both creativity and language learning. I took on multiple roles in the project: director, editor, co-writer, and performer. Together, we wrote lyrics using basic Mandarin vocabulary and grammar, set them to a beat, and produced a full video.


  • Language Application: Using beginner-level Mandarin in a real, creative context.
  • Collaboration: Coordinating responsibilities and ideas among a small group.
  • Performance & Confidence: Acting and performing in Mandarin on camera.

11th Grade Project

Amendment Podcast

As part of a collaborative assignment, I worked with three classmates to create a podcast called "Amendment", where we explained and analyzed a U.S. Constitutional amendment to educate our class. The project was designed to make complex civics content engaging through humor and discussion.


  • Co-Writing: Helping draft the script to balance both educational and comedic elements.
  • Co-Starring: Recording and performing as part of the podcast team, engaging the audience with humor and clear explanations.
